Volunteer Role Descriptions

  • Parking lot attendant: Guide visitors upon arrival
  • Docent: Educate the public about the schedule of activities
  • Public Concierge: Greet visitors and provide directions
  • Exhibitor Concierge: Help exhibitors set up and support them throughout the expo
  • PR Concierge: Greet and guide VIPs and Media
  • Go Bag stuffers: ON AUGUST 6, stuff 2,000 go bags with gear
  • Crowd control monitors: Wear safety vest and keep visitors out of areas that are closed to the public
